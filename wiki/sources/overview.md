The central narrative revolves around Minh Duy, a highly skilled AI Engineer and entrepreneur who has recently relocated to Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His professional focus is the intersection of advanced AI (Deep Learning, ML) and critical infrastructure security, specifically quantifying threats from Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Simultaneously, he is building a personal venture, Lowkie, in the fitness/food sector. His interests span advanced technology, entrepreneurship, and monitoring the Vietnamese financial markets, painting a picture of a highly adaptable, multi-faceted professional navigating both the tech and commercial landscapes of Southeast Asia.
