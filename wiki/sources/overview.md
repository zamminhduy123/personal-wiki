## Overview

This wiki tracks the professional and personal journey of Minh Duy (Rzy), an AI Engineer and entrepreneur based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His core professional focus is applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify risks associated with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is actively building a brand, Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack, and maintaining a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market.
