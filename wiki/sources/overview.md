## Overview
This wiki tracks the professional and personal journey of Minh Duy (Rzy), a highly skilled AI Engineer and Research Engineer. His work centers on applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to solve complex, real-world problems, particularly in the domain of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is actively engaged in entrepreneurship, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. The narrative spans his academic achievements (Master's degree from Soonchunhyang University) and his current life in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, where he monitors the local financial markets. The overall theme is the intersection of cutting-edge AI research, practical software development, and personal entrepreneurial ventures.
