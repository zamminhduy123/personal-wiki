## Overview

This wiki tracks the professional and personal journey of Minh Duy (Rzy), a highly specialized AI Engineer and entrepreneur. His core expertise lies at the intersection of advanced AI (Deep Learning, ML) and critical infrastructure security, specifically quantifying threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is an active entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese financial market. The narrative shows a recent professional pivot, marked by his relocation to Ho Chi Minh City in early 2026, following the completion of his Master's degree.
