Professionally, he is actively seeking AI Scientist/Engineer roles while simultaneously founding and managing Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. His interests span advanced technology, the Vietnamese stock market, and maintaining a dedicated fitness regimen. The narrative shows a clear progression from academic research (Soonchunhyang University) to practical, real-world application and entrepreneurship in Vietnam.
