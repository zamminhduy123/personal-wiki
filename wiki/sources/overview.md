## Overview

This wiki tracks the professional and personal journey of Minh Duy (Rzy), an AI Engineer and entrepreneur. The core narrative revolves around his transition into advanced AI research, specifically applying Deep Learning and Machine Learning to quantify risks associated with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Concurrently, he is building a business (Lowkie) and establishing roots in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, while maintaining a strong interest in the Vietnamese financial market.
