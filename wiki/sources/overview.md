## Overview

This wiki tracks the professional and personal journey of Minh Duy (Rzy), an AI specialist. The narrative centers on his transition from South Korea to Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, following the completion of his Master's degree in early 2026. His professional focus is highly specialized: applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify risks associated with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the SORA framework. Concurrently, he is building an entrepreneurial venture, Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, while maintaining a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market. The overall theme is one of rapid, multi-faceted professional and personal reinvention in the context of modern AI and Vietnamese commerce.
