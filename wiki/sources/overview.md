## Overview
This wiki tracks the professional and personal journey of Minh Duy (Rzy), a highly specialized AI Engineer and researcher. His work centers on applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify operational risks posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. The narrative spans his academic background (Soonchunhyang University) and his professional activities (Zalo, CT Group). Furthermore, the wiki documents his parallel interests in entrepreneurship, notably founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and his active monitoring of the Vietnamese stock market. The overall theme is the intersection of advanced AI research, practical engineering application, and personal entrepreneurial development within the context of Vietnam's rapidly developing tech and financial sectors.
