The central theme of this wiki revolves around the professional trajectory and specialized research of Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy (Minh Duy/Rzy). His work centers on applying advanced AI methodologies, specifically Deep Learning and Machine Learning, to solve complex, real-world problems. His primary research focus is quantifying the operational risks posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the structured SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, Minh Duy is actively engaged in entrepreneurship through Lowkie, a fitness-focused venture, and maintains diverse personal interests, including bodybuilding and monitoring the Vietnamese stock market. His professional life is marked by continuous movement and career exploration, transitioning from academic research (Soonchunhyang University) to establishing himself in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, while simultaneously building a brand and a business. This blend of high-tech specialization, entrepreneurial drive, and personal discipline defines his current narrative.
