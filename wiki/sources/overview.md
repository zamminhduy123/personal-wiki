The central narrative revolves around Minh Duy, a highly skilled AI Engineer and entrepreneur. His professional focus is the intersection of advanced AI (Deep Learning, ML) and critical security applications, specifically quantifying threats from Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Simultaneously, he is building a personal brand and business, Lowkie, in the fitness sector. His life trajectory is marked by academic achievement (Master's degree, Soonchunhyang University) and a geographical shift to Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, where he monitors the local financial markets. The overall theme is the transition from advanced academic research to practical, market-driven application in both technology and consumer goods.
