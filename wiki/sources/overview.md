The core narrative revolves around Minh Duy, a highly skilled AI Engineer and entrepreneur. His professional focus is the application of advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to solve critical real-world problems, specifically quantifying threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is actively building a brand (Lowkie) and managing his personal life, including relocation to Ho Chi Minh City and monitoring the Vietnamese financial markets. His journey highlights the intersection of advanced AI research, entrepreneurial ventures, and global mobility.
