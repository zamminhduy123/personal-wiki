## Overview

This wiki tracks the professional and personal journey of Minh Duy (Rzy), a specialist in AI and Machine Learning. His work centers on applying advanced computational methods, particularly Deep Learning, to quantify operational risks associated with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is an active entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market, demonstrating a multi-faceted profile spanning technology, business, and personal wellness.
