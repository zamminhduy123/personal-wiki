The core narrative revolves around Minh Duy, a highly skilled AI professional who is transitioning his deep technical expertise (Deep Learning, ML) into practical, high-impact domains. His primary focus is quantifying the operational risks posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the structured SORA framework. This technical specialization is coupled with a strong entrepreneurial drive, demonstrated by his founding of Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. Furthermore, his personal interests—monitoring the Vietnamese stock market and dedication to fitness—ground his professional ambitions within the economic and cultural context of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His career trajectory shows a move from academic research toward both advanced AI engineering roles and market-driven ventures.
