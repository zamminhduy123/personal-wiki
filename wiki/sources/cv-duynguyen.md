## Summary
Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy is a Graduate Research Assistant and former Software Engineer with expertise in applying deep learning for anomaly detection in in-vehicle networks. His background combines strong production engineering skills (2 years at Zalo) with advanced research capabilities in areas like Few-Shot Learning, Open-Set Recognition, and Generative AI. He has experience building scalable, multi-modal AI systems and robust RAG pipelines.

## Key Facts
- **Specialization:** Deep learning for anomaly detection in in-vehicle networks (data-scarce, open-set, adversarial settings).
- **Experience:** Software Engineer at Zalo (VNG Corporation, 2022-2024) and Graduate Research Assistant at Soonchunhyang University (2024-2026).
- **Technical Skills:** PyTorch, PyTorch Geometric (PyG), Generative AI, Multi-modal ML (CLIP/SigLIP), Transformers, GNN, Python, React.js, FastAPI, Docker.
- **Education:** Master of Science in Engineering (Soonchunhyang University); B.Sc. in Software Engineering (HCM VNU - University of Science).
- **Key Projects:** Interactive Video Retrieval System (CLIP/SigLIP, FAISS), RAG chatbot for academic center (FastAPI, RAG stack).
- **Publications:** Co-authored work on Multi-Class Intrusion Detection System for In-Vehicle Networks (Knowledge-Based Systems, Q1).
