## Summary
Minh Duy is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. Beyond his technical career, he is an entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. He maintains a strong interest in fitness and the Vietnamese stock market.

- **Name/Aliases:** Minh Duy (Rzy).
- **Current Location:** Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
