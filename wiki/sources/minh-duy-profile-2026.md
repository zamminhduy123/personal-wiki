## Summary
Minh Duy, also known as Rzy, is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. His professional life is balanced with active entrepreneurship, notably founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. He maintains a strong interest in the Vietnamese stock market and is actively pursuing AI roles.
