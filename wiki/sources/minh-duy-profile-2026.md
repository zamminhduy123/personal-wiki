## Summary
Minh Duy is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His professional focus is applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical work, he is an active entrepreneur, having founded Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market.
