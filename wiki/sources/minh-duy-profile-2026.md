## Summary
Minh Duy is a highly skilled AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His core expertise lies in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. Beyond his technical career, he is an active entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack initiative, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market.
