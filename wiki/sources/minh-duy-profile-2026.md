## Summary
Minh Duy is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer specializing in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). He is currently based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, and is actively pursuing AI Scientist/Engineer roles. Beyond his technical work, he is an entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market.
