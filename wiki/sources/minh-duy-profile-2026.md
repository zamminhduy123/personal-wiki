## Summary
Minh Duy, also known as Rzy, is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. His background includes a Master's degree from Soonchunhyang University (graduated Feb 2026) and professional experience at companies like Zalo. Beyond AI, he is an entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack initiative, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market.
