## Summary
Minh Duy, also known as Rzy, is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His professional focus is applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is an entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market.
