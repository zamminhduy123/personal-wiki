## Summary
Minh Duy is a highly specialized AI Engineer and researcher, currently based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His expertise lies in applying deep learning and ML to quantify threats for Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is an entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market.
