## Summary
Minh Duy is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer specializing in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). He is actively building a career in AI, having recently completed his Master's degree in February 2026.
