## Summary
Minh Duy, also known as Rzy, is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. His professional life is characterized by active job searching in AI roles, alongside entrepreneurial ventures like Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. He maintains a strong interest in fitness and monitoring the Vietnamese stock market.

## Key Facts
- **Name/Aliases:** Minh Duy (Rzy).
- **Current Location:** Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
- **Education:** Master’s degree (graduated February 2026) from Soonchunhyang University.
- **Primary Roles:** AI Engineer / Research Engineer.
- **Specialization:** Quantifying UAV threats using ML/DL within the SORA framework.
