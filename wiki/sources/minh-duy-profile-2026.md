## Summary
Minh Duy is a highly skilled AI Engineer and Research Engineer. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. He is an active entrepreneur, having founded Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. He is currently based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
