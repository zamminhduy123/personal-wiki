## Summary
Minh Duy is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer specializing in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. He is an active entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack initiative. He is highly interested in the Vietnamese stock market and is currently based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
