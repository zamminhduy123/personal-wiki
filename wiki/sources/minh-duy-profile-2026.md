## Summary
Minh Duy (Rzy) is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA framework. Beyond his technical career, he is an entrepreneur, founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. He maintains a strong interest in fitness and actively monitors the Vietnamese stock market.
