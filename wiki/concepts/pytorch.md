## Definition
PyTorch is an open-source machine learning framework widely used for deep learning research and production. It is known for its dynamic computation graph and ease of use.

## How It Applies Here
It is the primary framework used by Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy for developing and implementing advanced deep learning models (Transformers, GNNs) for intrusion detection.
