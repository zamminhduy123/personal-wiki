## Definition
Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning that uses artificial neural networks with multiple layers (deep architectures) to learn complex patterns directly from raw data. It is foundational to modern AI applications.

## How It Applies Here
It is the core methodology used by Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy, particularly in researching and applying models (Transformers, GNNs) for anomaly detection in complex systems like in-vehicle networks.
