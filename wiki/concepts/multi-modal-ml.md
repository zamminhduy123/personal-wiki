## Definition
Multi-modal Machine Learning involves models that can process, understand, and generate information from multiple types of data simultaneously (e.g., text, images, audio, video).

## How It Applies Here
Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy applied multi-modal AI (CLIP/SigLIP) for text-to-keyframe matching in video retrieval, demonstrating proficiency in handling diverse data types.
