## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It helps determine the necessary safety and security measures based on the operational context.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the primary context for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify and model potential threats posed by UAVs within specific operational environments.
