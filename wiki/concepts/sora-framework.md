## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The framework guides the quantification of threats in specific operational contexts.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy is specializing in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify threats for UAVs specifically within the SORA framework. This represents a critical intersection of AI research and defense/security assessment.
