## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured way to quantify and manage potential threats.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy specializes in applying the SORA framework. His work involves using Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify the specific threats posed by UAVs, allowing for better risk mitigation and operational planning in the field of security and defense.
