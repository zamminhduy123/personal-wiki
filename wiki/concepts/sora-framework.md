## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The framework guides the systematic identification and quantification of potential threats.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy is applying the SORA framework in his research, specifically using Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify the threats posed by UAVs. This application links advanced AI methodologies to critical security and risk assessment domains.
