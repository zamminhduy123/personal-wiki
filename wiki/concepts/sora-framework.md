## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured way to quantify threats and assess operational safety.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework in his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify the specific threats posed by UAVs, thereby contributing to operational risk mitigation in the aerospace domain.
