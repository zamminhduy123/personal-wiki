## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It guides the determination of necessary safety and security measures based on the operational context.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy applies the SORA framework in his research, using Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify and assess the operational threats posed by UAVs. This application moves the framework from theoretical risk assessment into a data-driven, quantifiable engineering domain.
