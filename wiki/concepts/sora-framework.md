## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic way to quantify threats and determine necessary mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy is applying the SORA framework to his research, specifically using Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify the operational risks and threats posed by UAVs in various scenarios. This application bridges advanced AI research with critical aerospace security assessment.
