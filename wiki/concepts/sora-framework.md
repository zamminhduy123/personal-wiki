## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess the operational risks associated with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It helps determine the necessary mitigation measures and operational constraints for safe deployment.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the primary structure for his research, applying ML/DL techniques to quantify and model the specific threats posed by UAVs, moving beyond general risk assessment to actionable threat quantification.
