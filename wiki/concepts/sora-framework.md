## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured way to quantify threats and determine necessary mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the core methodology for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify the specific threats posed by UAVs. This positions his work at the intersection of AI, risk assessment, and aerospace technology.
