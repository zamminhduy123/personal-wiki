## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used for assessing operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic way to evaluate the potential threats and required mitigation measures for drone operations.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify and assess the operational risks posed by UAVs. This application is central to his professional specialization.
