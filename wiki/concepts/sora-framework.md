## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used for assessing operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured way to evaluate potential threats and required mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework in his research to quantify the threats posed by UAVs. This application integrates Machine Learning and Deep Learning to assess operational risks in specific environments.
