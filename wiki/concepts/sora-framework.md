## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a framework for quantifying potential threats and necessary mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats within the SORA framework, specifically concerning UAV operations. This application bridges advanced AI research with critical security and risk assessment domains.
