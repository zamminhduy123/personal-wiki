## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured way to quantify potential threats and risks in specific operational environments.

## How It Applies Here
