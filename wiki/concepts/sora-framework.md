## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a framework for quantifying potential threats.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy specializes in applying ML/DL techniques within the SORA framework to quantify threats posed by UAVs. This methodology is central to his current research and professional focus.
