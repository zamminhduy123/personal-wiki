## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured way to evaluate the potential impact and severity of operational failures.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify and assess the threats posed by UAVs. This application helps in developing robust risk mitigation strategies for aerial operations.
