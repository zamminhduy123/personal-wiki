## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The framework is central to Minh Duy's current research focus.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy applies ML/DL techniques within the SORA framework to quantify and assess the threats posed by UAVs. This involves using advanced data analysis to model potential risks in specific operational environments.
