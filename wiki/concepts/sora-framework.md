## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) is a structured methodology used to assess the operational risks associated with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a framework for quantifying threats and determining necessary mitigation strategies for airspace integration.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the core methodology for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify specific threats posed by UAVs. This work is critical for advancing the safe integration of UAVs into complex airspace environments.
