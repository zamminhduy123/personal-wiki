## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It helps quantify the potential threat level and necessary mitigation strategies for specific operational environments.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify the threats posed by UAVs. This application moves the assessment from theoretical risk modeling to data-driven, quantifiable threat assessment.
