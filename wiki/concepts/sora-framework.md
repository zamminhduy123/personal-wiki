## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic way to quantify potential threats and required mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify the specific threats posed by UAVs. This application moves the framework from a theoretical risk assessment tool into a data-driven, predictive model.
