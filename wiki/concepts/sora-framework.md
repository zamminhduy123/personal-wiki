## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a framework for quantifying potential threats and risks in specific operational environments.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the core methodology for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by UAVs. This positions his work at the intersection of AI, risk assessment, and aerospace technology.
