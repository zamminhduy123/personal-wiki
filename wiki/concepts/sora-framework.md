## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess the operational risks associated with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured approach for evaluating potential threats and mitigating risks in specific operational environments.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the core methodology for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify the specific threats posed by UAVs. This positions his work at the intersection of AI, risk assessment, and aerospace technology.
