## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a framework for quantifying threats and developing mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework in his research to quantify threats posed by UAVs, integrating Machine Learning and Deep Learning models to assess operational risks in security systems.
