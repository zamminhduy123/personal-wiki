## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic approach for quantifying potential threats and defining mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the core structure for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify specific threats posed by UAVs. This positions his work at the intersection of advanced AI and critical infrastructure security.
