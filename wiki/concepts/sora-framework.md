## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess the risks associated with specific operational scenarios, often in high-risk environments like those involving UAVs.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy's research is centered on quantifying threats for UAVs *within* the SORA framework. This suggests his work involves modeling and predicting operational risks associated with drone deployment.
