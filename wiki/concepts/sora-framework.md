## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a framework for systematically identifying, analyzing, and mitigating potential hazards.

## How It Applies Here
This framework is the core methodology guiding Minh Duy's research. He applies ML/DL techniques within the SORA structure to quantify and assess the specific threats posed by UAVs in operational environments.
