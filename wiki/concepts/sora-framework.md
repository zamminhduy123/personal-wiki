## Definition
Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a framework for quantifying potential threats and required mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify the specific threats posed by UAVs, thereby contributing to aviation safety and risk management.
