## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The framework provides a systematic way to evaluate potential threats and vulnerabilities in specific operational contexts.
