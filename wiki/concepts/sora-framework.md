## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a structured approach for quantifying potential threats and required mitigation measures.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify the threats posed by UAVs. This positions his work at the intersection of AI, risk assessment, and aerospace technology.
