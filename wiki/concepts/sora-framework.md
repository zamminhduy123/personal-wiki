## Definition
The Structured Methodology for Assessing Operational Risks (SORA) is a structured framework used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic way to evaluate potential threats and vulnerabilities in operational environments.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the core methodology for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify specific threats posed by UAVs. This positions his work at the intersection of advanced AI and critical infrastructure risk assessment.
