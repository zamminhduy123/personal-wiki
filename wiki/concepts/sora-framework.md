## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly in the context of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic way to determine the necessary safety and security measures based on the operational environment and potential threats.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy specializes in applying ML/DL techniques to quantify threats specifically within the SORA framework. This involves using data science to model and assess the risks posed by UAVs in various operational scenarios, moving beyond simple threat identification to quantitative risk assessment.
