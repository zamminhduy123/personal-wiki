## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs).

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy is applying the SORA framework to quantify threats posed by UAVs using advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques. This positions his research at the intersection of AI and defense/security assessment.
