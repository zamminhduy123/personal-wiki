## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The framework is central to Minh Duy's research specialization.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy applies the SORA framework by using Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify potential threats posed by UAVs, thereby assessing operational risk in specific environments.
