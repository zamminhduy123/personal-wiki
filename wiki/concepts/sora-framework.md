## Definition
The SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic way to evaluate potential threats and vulnerabilities in specific operational environments.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the structure for his research. His work involves applying advanced AI techniques (ML/DL) to quantify the specific threats and risks associated with UAV operations, thereby providing a data-driven risk assessment.
