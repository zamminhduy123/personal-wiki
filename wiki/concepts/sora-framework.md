## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It helps quantify the threat level and required mitigation strategies for aerial operations.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify potential threats posed by UAVs. This positions his work at the intersection of risk assessment and advanced AI.
