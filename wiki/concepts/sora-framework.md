## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It helps quantify the threat level and required mitigation strategies for aerial systems.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy specializes in applying ML/DL techniques to quantify threats specifically within the SORA framework. This involves using data-driven methods to assess the operational risks posed by UAVs, moving beyond simple threat identification to structured risk quantification.
