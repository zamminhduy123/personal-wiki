## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It guides the quantification of threats and necessary mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy utilizes the SORA framework as the basis for his research, applying ML/DL techniques to quantify specific threats posed by UAVs, thereby defining the scope and severity of potential risks in operational environments.
