## Definition
SORA stands for Specific Operations Risk Assessment. It is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The framework is applied to quantify threats and ensure safe deployment of such technology.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques within the SORA framework. His research focuses on using these computational methods to quantify the threats posed by UAVs, making the assessment process more data-driven and precise.
