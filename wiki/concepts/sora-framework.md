## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It provides a systematic way to evaluate potential threats and required mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy applies the SORA framework in his research, using Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify the operational risks posed by UAVs. This application helps in developing defensive measures and understanding threat levels in specific operational environments.
