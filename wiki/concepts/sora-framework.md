## Definition
The Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework is a structured methodology used to assess operational risks, particularly those involving Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). It helps quantify potential threats and guide mitigation strategies.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy is applying the SORA framework in his research, using Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify the specific threats posed by UAVs. This application moves the framework from theoretical risk assessment into a data-driven, predictive model.
