## Definition
Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs), commonly known as drones, are aircraft that operate without a human pilot onboard. They are central to modern surveillance, delivery, and military applications, but also pose significant operational risks.

## How It Applies Here
UAVs are the primary subject of Minh Duy's research. He focuses on quantifying the threats posed by these vehicles, particularly within the context of the SORA framework, using advanced ML/DL techniques.
