## Definition
Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs), commonly known as drones, are aircraft that operate without a human pilot onboard. They are central to modern surveillance, delivery, and military applications, making them a focus of current security research.

## How It Applies Here
Minh Duy's research focuses specifically on quantifying the threats posed by UAVs. This involves using advanced ML/DL techniques to analyze data and predict potential operational risks associated with these vehicles, often within the context of the SORA framework.
