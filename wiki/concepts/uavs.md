## Definition
UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) are aircraft that operate without a human pilot onboard. They are used in various applications, including surveillance, mapping, military operations, and research.

## How It Applies Here
UAVs are central to Minh Duy's current research focus. His work involves using advanced ML/DL techniques to quantify the threats these vehicles pose, particularly when assessed through the lens of the SORA framework. This research is critical for improving airspace safety and security.
