## Definition
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the concept of 'Ăn ngon, tập khỏe' (Eating well, training well).

## How It Applies Here
Lowkie represents Minh Duy's entrepreneurial venture, demonstrating his interests outside of pure AI research. It connects his interest in fitness and nutrition with a commercial product.
