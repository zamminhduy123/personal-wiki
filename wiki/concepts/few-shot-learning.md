## Definition
Few-Shot Learning (FSL) is a machine learning paradigm where a model must learn to generalize from very limited examples (few shots) of a new class, rather than requiring thousands of labeled examples.

## How It Applies Here
Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy applied FSL to tackle highly imbalanced datasets and zero-day network attacks in in-vehicle networks, demonstrating ability to perform robust classification with minimal training data.
