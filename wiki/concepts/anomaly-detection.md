## Definition
Anomaly Detection is the process of identifying rare items, events, or observations that deviate significantly from the majority of the data. It is crucial for security and system health monitoring.

## How It Applies Here
This is a primary area of expertise for Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy, specifically applied to detecting intrusions and unknown/unseen classes (zero-day attacks) in in-vehicle CAN BUS networks.
