## Definition
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) Systems enhance Large Language Models (LLMs) by first retrieving relevant external documents or data chunks before generating a response. This grounds the output in verifiable information.

## How It Applies Here
Nguyen Thanh Minh Duy has built and implemented compact RAG stacks for chatbots, utilizing vector search and validator-based prompting to minimize hallucinations.
