## Overview
Minh Duy (also known as Rzy) is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. He is an active entrepreneur and investor, managing a fitness-focused snack brand (Lowkie) and monitoring the Vietnamese stock market.
