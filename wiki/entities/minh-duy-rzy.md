## Overview
Minh Duy (also known as Rzy) is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. He is an active entrepreneur and has relocated to Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

- **Career Focus:** Actively seeking AI Scientist/Engineer roles.
