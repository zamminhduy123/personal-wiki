## Overview
Minh Duy, also known by the alias Rzy, is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. He is actively building a career in AI while managing personal ventures, including founding Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. He maintains a strong interest in the Vietnamese stock market and fitness.
