## Overview
Minh Duy, also known by the alias Rzy, is a specialized AI Engineer and researcher. He is currently based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. His professional focus involves applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework. He is also an active entrepreneur, having founded Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack initiative.
