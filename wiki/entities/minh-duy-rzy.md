## Overview
Minh Duy, also known as Rzy, is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He specializes in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats for Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the SORA framework. He holds a Master's degree from Soonchunhyang University and has a background in software engineering and practical automation. Beyond his technical career, he is a founder of Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand, and maintains a strong interest in the Vietnamese financial markets.
