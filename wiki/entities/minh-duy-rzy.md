## Overview

He specializes in applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework. He is an active entrepreneur, having founded Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack initiative. He is currently based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, and is actively monitoring the Vietnamese stock market.
