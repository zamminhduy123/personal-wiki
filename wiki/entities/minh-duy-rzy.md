## Overview
Minh Duy, also known by the alias Rzy, is a highly skilled AI Engineer and Research Engineer. He specializes in applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment) framework. He is also an active entrepreneur, having founded Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand.
