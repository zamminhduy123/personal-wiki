## Overview
Minh Duy, also known by the alias Rzy, is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. He is highly specialized in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). He is also an entrepreneur, having founded Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack venture.
