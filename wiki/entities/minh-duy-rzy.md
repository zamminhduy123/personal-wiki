## Overview
Minh Duy, also known by the alias Rzy, is a highly skilled AI Engineer and Research Engineer. He specializes in applying advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). He is an active entrepreneur, having founded Lowkie, a fitness-focused snack brand. He is currently based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, and maintains a keen interest in the Vietnamese stock market and fitness.
