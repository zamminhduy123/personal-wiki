## Overview
Minh Duy (also known as Rzy) is an AI Engineer and Research Engineer specializing in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). He is actively involved in the tech industry, pursuing AI Scientist/Engineer roles, and is a founder of the fitness-focused snack brand, Lowkie. He is based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

## Key Facts
- **Aliases:** Rzy.
- **Current Focus:** Quantifying UAV threats using ML/DL within the SORA framework.
- **Location:** Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
- **Education:** Master's degree from Soonchunhyang University (completed Feb 2026).
- **Venture:** Founder of Lowkie.
- **Interests:** Monitoring the Vietnamese stock market (energy, utility, steel sectors).
- **Timeline:** Relocated to HCMC in April 2026.
