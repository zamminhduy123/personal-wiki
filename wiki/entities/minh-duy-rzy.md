## Overview
Minh Duy (also known as Rzy) is a highly specialized AI Engineer and researcher. He focuses on applying advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques to quantify threats posed by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) within the Specific Operations Risk Assessment (SORA) framework. He is an active entrepreneur and maintains interests in fitness and the Vietnamese financial markets.
