## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing and marketing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eating well, training hard).

## Key Facts
- **Product:** Protein-enriched cookies.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
- **Focus:** Fitness and nutrition.
- **Development:** Baking and formulation experiments conducted in late 2025.
