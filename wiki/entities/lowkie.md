## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. Its primary goal is to develop and market protein-enriched cookies, operating under the slogan "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eat well, train strong).

## Key Facts
- Founder: Minh Duy.
- Product Focus: Protein-enriched cookies.
- Mission: To provide healthy, delicious snacks for fitness enthusiasts.
- Status: Active venture, involving baking and formulation experiments.
