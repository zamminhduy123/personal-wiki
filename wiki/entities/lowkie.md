## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing and selling protein-enriched cookies, marketed under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Delicious to eat, healthy to train).

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Protein-enriched cookie brand.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
- **Focus:** Fitness and nutrition, catering to muscle gain and definition.
- **Activity:** Involved daily baking and formulation experiments (late 2025).
