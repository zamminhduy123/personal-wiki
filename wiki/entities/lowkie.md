## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. The brand specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the motto of promoting both physical health and enjoyment ("Ăn ngon, tập khỏe").

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Fitness/Food Product Initiative.
- **Focus:** Protein-enriched cookies.
- **Motto:** "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Enjoy eating, train healthily).
- **Activity:** Involved daily baking and formulation experiments in late 2025.
