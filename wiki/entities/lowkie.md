## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing and marketing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the motto "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eat well, train strong).

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Protein-enriched cookie brand/snack initiative.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
- **Focus:** Fitness and nutrition, targeting muscle gain and healthy eating.
- **Activity:** Involved in daily baking and formulation experiments (late 2025).
