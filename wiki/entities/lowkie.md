## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the concept of 'Ăn ngon, tập khỏe' (Eating well, training well).

## Key Facts
- **Focus:** Protein-enriched cookies/snacks.
- **Goal:** Supporting fitness and muscle gain.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
