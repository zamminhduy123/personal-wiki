## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. The brand aims to provide protein-enriched cookies, operating under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eating well, training hard).

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Snack/Food Product Initiative.
- **Focus:** Protein-enriched cookies.
- **Motto:** "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe".
- **Activity:** Involved daily baking and formulation experiments in late 2025.
