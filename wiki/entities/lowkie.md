## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. The brand focuses on developing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the slogan "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eat well, train strong).

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Fitness snack brand/project.
- **Focus:** Protein-enriched cookies.
- **Motto:** "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe".
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
- **Activity:** Involved in formulation and baking experiments (late 2025).
