## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies under the slogan "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eat well, train strong).

## Key Facts
- **Focus:** Protein-enriched cookies and snacks.
- **Goal:** To provide healthy, convenient nutrition for fitness enthusiasts.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
