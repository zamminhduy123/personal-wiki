## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eating well, training hard).

## Key Facts
- **Focus:** Protein-enriched cookies and fitness nutrition.
- **Goal:** To provide healthy, convenient snacks for fitness enthusiasts.
- **Status:** Active venture, with initial formulation experiments conducted in late 2025.
