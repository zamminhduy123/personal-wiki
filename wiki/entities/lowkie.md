## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. The brand aims to provide protein-enriched cookies, operating under the motto of promoting both physical health and enjoyment ("Ăn ngon, tập khỏe").

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Fitness snack brand/venture.
- **Product:** Protein-enriched cookies.
- **Goal:** Promoting physical health and nutrition.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
