## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. The brand specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies, operating under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eating well, training hard). It represents Minh Duy's entrepreneurial venture.

## Key Facts
- **Focus:** Protein-enriched cookies and fitness nutrition.
- **Motto:** "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe".
- **Status:** Active venture, with formulation experiments conducted in late 2025.
