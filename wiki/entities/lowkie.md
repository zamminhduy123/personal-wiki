## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. The brand aims to provide protein-enriched cookies, operating under the slogan "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eat well, train hard). It represents Minh Duy's entrepreneurial venture.

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Fitness-focused snack brand/project.
- **Goal:** Developing and marketing protein-enriched cookies.
- **Slogan:** "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eat well, train hard).
- **Founder:** Minh Duy (Rzy).
