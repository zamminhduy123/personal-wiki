## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing and selling protein-enriched cookies, marketed under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eating well, training hard).

## Key Facts
- **Nature:** Protein-enriched cookie brand.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
- **Focus:** Fitness and nutrition.
- **Goal:** To provide a healthy, high-protein snack option.
