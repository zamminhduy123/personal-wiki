## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies, marketed under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Delicious to eat, healthy to train).

## Key Facts
- **Product:** Protein-enriched cookies.
- **Goal:** To provide a nutritious snack supporting fitness and bodybuilding goals.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
- **Activity:** Involved daily baking and formulation experiments in late 2025.
