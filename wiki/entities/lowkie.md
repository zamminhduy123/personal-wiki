## Overview
Lowkie is a fitness-focused snack initiative founded by Minh Duy. It specializes in developing protein-enriched cookies, marketed under the concept of "Ăn ngon, tập khỏe" (Eating well, training well).

## Key Facts
- **Type:** Snack/Food Product.
- **Focus:** High protein content for fitness enthusiasts.
- **Founder:** Minh Duy.
- **Goal:** To provide a convenient, nutritious snack for those engaged in bodybuilding and fitness.
